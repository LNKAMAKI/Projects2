What is Antarctica like?

Characteristics:

- It's the world's highest continent

- It has the coldest climate in the world

- About 70% of the Earth's fresh water is found in Antarctica

- It is located in the Southern Hemisphere

- It has a surface area of 14.2 million km2

- It is Larger than Europe

- In the winter, the water around Antarctica freezes and the continent doubles in size

- A few species of animals, like penguins and seals, live along the coast of Antarctica
